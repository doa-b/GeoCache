General info

Geocaching is a real-world, outdoor treasure hunting game using GPS-enabled devices. Participants navigate to a specific set of GPS coordinates and then attempt to find the geocache (container) hidden at that location. With this app you can register as many new geocaches as you desire. You can then share your database with other users of this app so they can find your treasures. You can also import databases from other users to search for new caches yourself!

 

Google Maps View

Normal Google map controls apply.

The cache icons represents the cache type. Click on a cache to display its information window. When you click on this window you will be taken to the detail page where you can view and edit all the cache’s information.

 

Menu

When you click on “list” you will be taken to a page with a list of all the caches. If you click on a list you will be taken to the detail page.

When you click on the “plus sign” you can enter a new cache on the detail page.

You can select “Export Database” to save your database to a location of choice to share it with your friends.

You can also “Import Database” to use caches that your friend has stored with the same App.
